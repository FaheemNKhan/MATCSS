Usage

Empty states displays a non-interactive image and a text tagline combined with an icon, title and description. Usually, they are enhancements for errors.

wifi_off

You're offline. Explore downloads?

Pick videos that will download automatically the next time you're online. 


<div class="empty-state">
    <div class="empty-state-container">
        <i class="material-icons icon">wifi_off</i>
        <h2 class="title">You are offline. Explore downloads?</h2>
        <p>
            Pick videos that will download automatically the next time you're
            online.
        </p>
        <button type="button" class="btn btn--raised">go to downloads</button>
        <button type="button" class="btn">retry</button>
    </div>
 </div>
